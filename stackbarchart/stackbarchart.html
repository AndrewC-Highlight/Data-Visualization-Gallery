The Stacked Bar Chart

This stacked bar chart is showing the overall amount of subscribers gained in months ranging from June - November 2019. It is subdivided into four categories of subscriber gains: core, print, crosswords, and audio.

Stacked Bar Chart Uses

The stacked bar chart acts as an "advanced" bar chart. It is able to stil show an overall dataset/independent variable, but with the added ability to visualize the sub-segments that comprise the overall segment. This can help visualize the relationship of the data and discern where, in this case, the subscribers are coming from.

Marks and Channels

  • Marks: Rectangles/Lines
  • Channels: Vertical Height, Color Hue
