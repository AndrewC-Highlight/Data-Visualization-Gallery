The Line Chart

This particular line chart analyzes the New York Times subscription timeline from 2011 to 2018. Of note, is a steady increase from 2011 to 2015, with a massive increase from 2016 to 2018. While this data in particular is fibbed, it should be noted that this increase is similar to the actual subscription numbers. In 2016, the "Trump Bump" ocurred, giving the Times and numerous other journalism outlets huge surges in subscriptions and users.

Line Chart Uses

Line charts are great to show the changes of a particular dataset over a period of time. They can measure short and long time changes, as the dates are typically found on the x-axis and the independent data on the y-axis.
