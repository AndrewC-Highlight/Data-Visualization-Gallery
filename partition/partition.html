Partition

Examining the hierarchy of sample news coverage categories of The New York Times

Partition

This partition chart utilizes the same dataset from the force and cluster chart, displaying the hierarchy of a sample of categories and subcategories for news coverage. It's similar to a tree diagram, utilizing parents/children to form, in this case a horizontal chart showcasing the hierarchy through descending rectangles. A legend was omitted, as the color hue is primarily used to help display the level in the hierarchy

Parititon Chart Uses

The partition chart is another way of displaying a hierarchy. Each layer here is subdivided depending on how many nodes are in that particular layer to fill in the hierarchy. It helps visualize the hierarchy in a uniform way, with clean divides and sizing improving the legibility. It shares similar uses from the cluster chart.

Marks and Channels

  • Marks: Rectangle, Connection (implied)
  • Channels: Color Hue, Area(2D size)
