The Donut Chart

This particular donut chart shows the same data as the pie chart: the number of different areas that subscribers were gained from for The New York Times in Q3 2019.

Donut Chart Uses

The donut chart is extremely similar to the pie chart. It serves two main differences though, in that it can be used to indicate more than one data series, as well as adding to the legibility of the data. In this particular chart, we are using the latter to provide a moderately better visualized chart.

Marks and Channels

  • Marks: Lines, Area
  • Channels: Color Hue, Area(2D size), Shape
