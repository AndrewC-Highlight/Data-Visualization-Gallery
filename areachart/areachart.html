The Area Chart

This area chart displays the same data as the line chart, and functions in a similar way. Similar to the line chart, the main purpose of this is to display the time and data relationship of total subscribers by year for The New York Times.

Area Chart Uses

The area chart is very similar to the line chart in displaying time and data changes/relationships.The main difference is that the area under the lines are filled in, helping to visualize the relationship and increases in this chart.

Marks and Channels

  • Marks: Line, Dots
  • Channels: Positions, Area
