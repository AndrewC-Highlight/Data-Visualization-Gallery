The Chord Chart

This chord chart analyzes users who switched their subscription to another media or journalism company. It shows how many people switched from Company A to Company B in the form of a chord chart. The data used is not real in the slightest. The values were simply generated randomly from 10-80.

Chord Chart Uses

The chord chart is a way to display relationships or network flows in a pleasing layout. It works well for bidirectional relationships, such as energy flows, resources, or economic data. The chords travel between categories and change in width depending on the values. The chord's source value determines the color here, letting you know which way the flow is greater from.

Marks and Channels
