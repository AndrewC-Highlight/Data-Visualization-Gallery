The Bar Chart

This particular bar chart analyzes the New York Times current subscription and user count, analyzing where the users are coming from.

Bar Chart Uses

Bar charts are useful to display measurements or frequencies of data for a specific category, maintaining a relative visual relationship. The y-axis in this case starts at 0 to get a full and uninterrupted idea of the difference between each categories subscriber/user number.

Marks and Channels

  • Marks: Rectangles/Line
  • Channels: Vertical Length, Color Hue
