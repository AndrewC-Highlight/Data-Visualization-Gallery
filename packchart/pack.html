Value

The Packed Circle Chart

The packed circle chart is another form of hierarchy chart, this time identifying how many views a category and its subcategory received on a specific day (this is also completely made up data). It takes a few categories such as trending, metro, and sports, with their subcategories and puts them into the hierarchy based on parents/children and values of the children.

Packed Circle Chart Uses

The packed circle chart is essentially a treemap but with circles. The circles are grouped inside each other as a level of hierarchy. The circles are sized in proportion to a value of their category, as colors help also to identify the grouping. It can be useful for various sizes of data, including large data sets where some legibility of smaller categories is sacrificed for a visually pleasant display of the larger connections and hierarchies. It is somewhat inefficient in spacing, but it does a good job of displaying the hierarchy.

Marks and Channels
