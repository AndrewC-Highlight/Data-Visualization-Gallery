The Scatter Plot

This particular scatter plot analyzes the relationship between the marketing budget spent for an individual subscriber campaign and the total subscribers gained by the end of that event.

Scatter Plot Uses

The scatter plot displays different data points with the aim to detail how one variable affects the other. The x-axis will measure one variable, while the y-axis measures another, and the dots are plotted to observe any apparent relationship.
